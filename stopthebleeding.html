The Hard Truth

Your rent is
someone else's
mortgage.

Manhattan rents have climbed for a decade. Meanwhile, your monthly payment builds exactly zero equity — zero ownership — zero wealth. At some point, the math stops making sense.

We're not here to pressure you. We're here to show you the numbers so you can make the call yourself.

$0
Equity you build from rent
Every check is gone the moment it clears. There's no asset, no return, no net worth being built.
30%
NYC rent increase over the past decade
Your landlord's payment to the bank didn't go up. Yours did.
$400K
Average decade of rent in NYC
Ten years of payments with nothing to show. That same money, toward a mortgage, builds real wealth.
Fixed
A mortgage payment never goes up
Unlike rent, a fixed-rate mortgage stays flat for 30 years — the day you close is the last time your housing cost surprises you.

What We Cover

01
The Real Math on Renting vs. Buying
Side-by-side comparison of what your money does in each scenario — including neighborhoods where a mortgage can cost less than rent right now.

How to Get Pre-Approved — and Why It Changes Everything
Chase Bank walks you through the pre-approval process, what lenders actually look at, and what assistance programs you may already qualify for.
03
The Legal Side of Buying in NYC
What happens between contract and closing, what to watch out for, and why NYC real estate law is different from everywhere else in the country.
04
Co-op vs. Condo: Which One Is Actually Right for You
NYC's unique ownership structures explained clearly — the real differences in access, board approval, financing, and long-term flexibility.
05
Down Payment Assistance You Didn't Know Existed
City, state, and federal programs that can reduce what you need upfront — most first-time buyers have no idea these exist.
06
How Representation Works
Working with a buyer’s agent means having one clear team guiding you through pricing, negotiation, due diligence, and board preparation. We’ll explain how compensation is structured, what to expect from the process, and why working with one dedicated representation team keeps everything aligned from offer to closing.
